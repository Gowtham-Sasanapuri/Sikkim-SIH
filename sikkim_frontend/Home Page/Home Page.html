Borders & Beauty: Sikkim

Discover Sikkim

Nestled in northeastern India, Sikkim is a land of breathtaking landscapes, vibrant culture, and warm hospitality. Despite its small size, it offers an incredible mix of nature, tradition, and adventure.

  • Himalayan beauty: Snow-capped peaks, serene valleys, lush forests, and waterfalls, including the majestic Kanchenjunga.
  • Buddhist heritage: Ancient monasteries and colorful festivals reflect Sikkim’s rich spiritual traditions.
  • Delicious cuisine: Savor momos, thukpa, gundruk, and other local delights.
  • Adventure & ecotourism: Trekking, river rafting, paragliding, and pristine lakes await nature lovers.
  • Unique wildlife: Spot rare species like the red panda and Himalayan monal.
